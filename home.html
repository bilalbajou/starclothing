ABOUT US

Founded by three partners of French origin in 1988, star clothing started its commercial life as a wholesale brand. Established and registered in Paris, star clothing attracted attention with its t-shirt and sweatshirt designs in the first place. With the separation of the two founding partners of the brand in time, star clothing was passed to George Amouyal as the sole shareholder. Tema Tekstil, which is connected to Taha Group and is the producer as well as licensor of LC Waikiki in Turkey, bought the worldwide rights of the star clothing brand in 1997.
